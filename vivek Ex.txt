Cool-Down (5-10 minutes):

Light stretching focusing on worked muscle groups


Warm-Up (5-10 minutes):

Light cardio and dynamic stretches


Workout:

Running or Jogging - 30 minutes at a moderate pace
OR
Cycling - 30-45 minutes at a moderate pace
OR
High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT):
1 minute high-intensity exercise (e.g., sprinting, fast cycling)
1 minute low-intensity recovery
Repeat for 20-30 minutes
